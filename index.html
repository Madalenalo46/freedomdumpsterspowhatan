Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rent a dumpster in an emergency?

Just call us. We prioritize urgent needs and can often arrange for same day dumpster rental. We’ll ask a few quick questions about your situation to recommend the right size and get a truck on the way.

How long can I keep the dumpster?

Our standard rental period is flexible. We understand that emergency cleanups can be unpredictable, so we will work with you on a timeline that gives you enough time to complete your work without pressure.

What happens when the dumpster is full?

Simply give us a call, and we will schedule a prompt pickup. If you fill it up and still have more to go, we can swap it out with an empty container to keep your project moving.

How long is the standard rental period?

Our typical rental period is 7-10 days, but we offer a great deal of flexibility. We understand that challenging cleanouts can take time, and we are happy to work with you on a schedule that fits your needs.

How much weight can I put in a dumpster?

The weight varies by the type of debris. General household junk is relatively light, while construction debris like concrete or roofing shingles is very heavy. Tell us about your project, and we can give you a good estimate.

What’s the safest way to load a dumpster?

Always wear gloves and sturdy shoes. Use the rear door to walk in heavy items instead of lifting them over the side. Load with a partner, and never stand on the edge of the dumpster walls.
